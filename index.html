My personal favourite thing in the entire universe. It hits differently every single time. One smile and whatever bad day I was having? Gone. Absolutely gone.

Her Personality

Equal parts silly, surprisingly deep, and dangerously cute. Every conversation with her feels like an adventure I never want to end.

She has big, brilliant, beautiful goals. And even on the days she doubts herself, I can see exactly how far she's going to go. She's going to conquer everything.

She has the softest, most generous heart. The way she cares for the people she loves — genuinely, deeply — makes the world a better place just by existing.
